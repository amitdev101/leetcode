In BNPL product allows for multiple purchases up to a credit limit (similar to a revolving credit account), the Credit Card Service calculates interest on any outstanding balances not paid off by the due date. LMS might still play a role if these balances eventually convert into a formal loan with fixed payments.
Even though the customer interacts with the BNPL service as if it were a single revolving credit account, the backend may consist of multiple distinct loans. This approach provides a balance between offering a seamless user experience and maintaining detailed control over the financial products offered to each customer
